When a firm decides to embrace AI and automation, the first instinct is often to try and automate the most complex parts of their professional work. However, the most successful implementations follow the 80/20 rule: 80% of your time savings will come from automating the "boring" 20% of your tasks.

At eleoro, we help firms identify these "low-hanging fruit" workflows that provide immediate ROI.

## 1. High-Volume, Low-Complexity Tasks

Look for the tasks your team does every single day that require almost zero professional judgment. This includes:
- Filing emails into case folders.
- Sending meeting reminders.
- Updating client contact information across multiple systems.
- Generating standard invoices.

These are "shallow work" tasks that fragment your team's focus. Automating them first provides an immediate "breathing room" for more complex projects.

## 2. Client Intake and Data Entry

If your staff is manually typing data from a paper form into a computer, that is a prime candidate for automation. Intake is often the single biggest time-sink in professional services. By moving to a "digital-first" intake workflow, you eliminate data entry errors and speed up the entire onboarding lifecycle.

## 3. Status Updates and Communication

"Where do we stand?" is the most common question clients ask. While answering it is important, doing so manually 50 times a week is inefficient. We build workflows that automatically pull status data from your project management tool and send a proactive weekly update to the client. This reduces inbound calls and makes the client feel prioritized.

## How to Start

We recommend starting with a single, high-impact workflow—usually intake or billing. This allows your team to get comfortable with the new system without overwhelming them. Once the first "win" is secured, we expand into the more nuanced layers of your practice.

Automation isn't an all-or-nothing proposition. It’s a journey toward a more efficient, profitable practice.
