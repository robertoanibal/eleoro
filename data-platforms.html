What We Do

We help you select and set up a customer data platform, define how profiles are matched, and build reusable segments. Consent and preferences are respected everywhere. Updates are proposed in a preview for sign-off before launch.

How It Works

  1. Inventory sources, destinations, and current segments.
  2. Choose match keys and matching rules.
  3. Model events, traits, and consent states.
  4. Validate in a sandbox, backfill history, then turn on channels.

Privacy & Safety

  • Careful handling of personal information
  • Region-aware storage and retention
  • Role-based access with audit logs

Key Benefits

  • One profile per person
  • Timely activation for key moments
  • Cleaner data for analysis and AI
  • Lower privacy risk
