What We Do

Modern marketing depends on a single view of the customer. We help you implement leading customer data platforms without endorsing specific vendors. Our process begins with designing event taxonomies and data governance policies so that data is captured consistently across your apps and sites.

We build pipelines that ingest behavioural events - product views, page visits, purchase frequency, cart items - along with offline data from your CRM and transactional systems. We then configure identity resolution rules to stitch together anonymous and known interactions, leveraging deterministic and probabilistic techniques to build a unified identity graph.

Once your profiles are unified, we integrate the CDP with your CRM, marketing automation tools, analytics platforms and ad networks. This allows you to create granular segments in real time and trigger personalised journeys everywhere. We also ensure data privacy compliance and governance so your customer information is secure and accurate.

Key Benefits

  • Consolidate data from web, mobile, CRM and offline sources
  • Create unified profiles with robust identity resolution
  • Deploy event schemas and governance frameworks
  • Activate segments across marketing and analytics platforms
