Voice Agent: Intelligent phone conversations that qualify, estimate and book

The Voice Agent answers calls, asks guided questions, retrieves data, generates estimates and books appointments automatically.

How Voice Automation Works

  • 1. Engage and Qualify

    The Voice Agent holds natural conversations, asks guided qualifying questions and captures the details needed for next steps.

  • 2. Retrieve Data Instantly

    It pulls pricing, service catalogues, order history, company policies and more to answer questions accurately in real time.

    The agent prepares estimates using your templates, checks calendar availability and books the appointment. A confirmation email is sent automatically.
