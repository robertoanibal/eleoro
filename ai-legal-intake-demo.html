Workflow Summary

This demo illustrates an AI-assisted intake workflow where routine steps are handled by an AI agent and reviewed by attorneys before finalization.

What the workflow handles

  • ✔️ Matters are prepared with structured metadata.
  • ✔️ Draft documents are organized and versioned.
  • ✔️ Workflow steps are logged for review.

Where time is reduced

  • ✔️ Fewer manual intake and document preparation steps.
  • ✔️ Less rework caused by missing or inconsistent data.
  • ✔️ Reduced coordination between systems.

How this scales

  • ✔️ Intake volume can increase without added staff.
  • ✔️ Review checkpoints remain consistent.
  • ✔️ Oversight is preserved as workload grows.

Attorney Oversight

  • ✔️ AI agents handle administrative tasks, freeing attorneys to focus on legal strategy.
  • ✔️ Every action is transparent and subject to attorney review.
  • ✔️ Clients receive consistent communications with attorney approval.
