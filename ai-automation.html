What We Do

Generative AI is transforming the way marketing teams create, test and optimise their messaging. At eleoro we develop custom GPT agents and content pipelines that generate subject lines, ad copy, SMS messages and chat responses on demand. We also design decisioning frameworks that utilise machine‑learning models to select the best variation for each audience and deliver it through the right channel at the right time.

Beyond content generation, we implement predictive models for lead scoring, churn risk, next‑best action and product affinity. These models help you prioritise resources and personalise experiences at scale. To ensure ongoing performance gains, our AI testing frameworks automatically experiment with creative, timing and channel combinations and learn what resonates with your customers.

Automation ties it all together. Using a combination of integration platforms and custom scripts, we orchestrate complex, cross‑system workflows that eliminate manual steps. From triggering journeys when a prospect downloads a white paper to updating scores and segments when a purchase is made, our automations keep your campaigns running smoothly.

Key Benefits

  • Accelerate content production with generative AI
  • Improve targeting through predictive models and real‑time decisioning
  • Continuously optimise campaigns via automated experimentation
  • Reduce manual workload by orchestrating cross‑platform processes
