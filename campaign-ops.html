What We Do

Connecting with your audience means delivering the right message at the right moment in the right place. We help you plan and execute full‑funnel campaigns across email, SMS, push notifications, in‑app messaging and paid media. Our strategists define triggers and conditions based on behaviours like first purchase, repeat visits, high‑value segments and churn signals, and map touchpoints across channels.

We then build modular automations using leading journey builders and workflow automation tools. Our workflows support A/B and multivariate experiments with control groups so you can learn what works while keeping a clean customer experience. To ensure quality, we establish governance, QA processes and cross‑functional playbooks that keep all stakeholders aligned and campaigns running smoothly.

Finally, we provide programme management and stakeholder alignment across marketing, product, analytics and IT. This includes training internal teams, managing vendor and offshore resources and ensuring that your campaigns adhere to brand, legal and compliance guidelines.

Key Benefits

  • Orchestrate campaigns across email, SMS, push and paid media
  • Trigger journeys based on real‑time customer behaviour
  • Experiment and optimise with built‑in testing frameworks
  • Establish governance and processes for consistent execution
