Privacy Policy for eleoro

Effective date: October 14, 2025
Last updated: December 29, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how eleoro ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you visit our website, use our services, or otherwise interact with us. It applies to all visitors, users, and customers ("you", "your") of eleoro.com and related services. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting information in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

2. Scope and Acceptance

By accessing or using our website or services, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services or provide information to us.

3. Definitions

Personal Information means any information that identifies or could reasonably identify an individual, such as a name, email address, contact details, identifiers, or online identifiers.

Services means any products, tools, features, or resources offered by eleoro.

4. Information We Collect

eleoro does not host or independently retain long-term customer data from connected platforms. Information that may be accessed or temporarily handled during configuration, setup, or support includes:

  • API keys or connection tokens provided temporarily for integrations
  • Sample or demonstration data used for configuration and testing
  • Contact names, job titles, email addresses, and phone numbers for client communications

We do not collect personal information through advertising, tracking, or remarketing tools.

5. How We Use Information

Information is used solely to:

  • Configure, integrate, and maintain automated workflows
  • Provide support and troubleshoot technical issues
  • Communicate regarding service updates, support requests, or account matters

Information is not used for advertising, profiling, or resale.

6. Legal Bases for Processing

Where applicable under laws such as the GDPR, information is processed based on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Consent
  • Performance of a contract
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Legitimate business interests related to service delivery and improvement

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

eleoro uses minimal cookies necessary for basic website functionality and limited analytics. These cookies do not enable behavioral advertising or cross-site tracking. You may disable cookies through your browser settings.

8. Third-Party Services

As part of service delivery, eleoro may interact with third-party platforms such as automation providers, cloud infrastructure services, and AI model providers. These platforms operate under their own privacy policies and data protection practices.

eleoro does not sell personal information and does not share information for third-party marketing purposes. Information may be disclosed only:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect rights, property, or safety
  • To deliver services under confidentiality obligations

9. Data Retention

eleoro does not retain customer data beyond the duration required for configuration, testing, or support. Credentials, tokens, and temporary files are deleted once setup is complete or upon written request. Data retained within third-party platforms is governed by those providers.

10. International Data Transfers

Data flows directly between connected platforms according to their own controls. Where international transfers occur, they are governed by the safeguards and transfer mechanisms implemented by those platforms. eleoro does not independently export or transfer personal data.

11. Security Measures

Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are used to protect information during processing, including encryption in transit, access controls, and reliance on reputable infrastructure providers.

12. Your Rights

Depending on jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of personal information. Because eleoro does not store personal data long-term, requests should generally be directed to the platform that controls the data. eleoro will confirm whether any residual data exists and assist as appropriate.

13. Children's Privacy

Services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. eleoro does not knowingly collect information from children.

14. Incident Response

If a security incident affecting supported configurations is identified, eleoro will notify the designated contact without undue delay and provide reasonable assistance with investigation and remediation.

15. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on eleoro.com/privacy with a revised effective date.
