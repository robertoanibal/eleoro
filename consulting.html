What We Do

Agents assist humans - not replace them. They start read-only, learn your data model, and propose changes as dry-run diffs or pull requests. You review and approve; then agents apply and log changes.

How It Works (Agent-Assisted)

  1. Connect with least-privilege access (read-only first).
  2. Map entities, events, and business rules.
  3. Propose changes as diffs/PRs or SDK calls in dry-run mode.
  4. You approve; agents apply and audit every change.

Privacy & Safety

  • Your data, prompts, and embeddings never train public models.
  • Payload logging is off by default or sent to your SIEM under your retention.
  • RAG first; fine-tuning only when justified and within your boundary.

We’re vendor-agnostic. Whether it’s a CDP, a marketing automation platform, a CRM, or a data warehouse, agents connect the dots so audiences, journeys, and analytics stay in sync.

Key Benefits

  • Select the right vendors and manage relationships effectively
  • Align stakeholders around shared goals and roadmaps
  • Ensure compliance and governance at enterprise scale
