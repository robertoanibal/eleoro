Follow-Up Agent: Automate reminders and updates so every customer feels valued and no opportunity is missed

Deliver timely, personalized messages across email, SMS, and chat — from confirmations and proposal nudges to review requests and payment reminders. Each interaction is logged in your CRM and escalated only when a human touch is truly needed.

How Follow-Up Works

  • 1. Listen & Capture

    The agent constantly scans emails, forms, calendar events, and chats to catch any new customer inquiry or scheduled follow-up opportunity.

  • 2. Decide & Schedule

    Using predefined rules, it picks the right channel and template for the message, sets the follow-up cadence, and schedules the next touchpoint.

  • 3. Engage & Log

    It sends out the email, SMS, or chat message at the perfect time and logs all activities in your CRM. If a customer responds or an issue arises, your team is alerted immediately.
