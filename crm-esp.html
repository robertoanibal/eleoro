What We Do

A robust CRM and messaging platform are at the heart of every customer engagement strategy. We migrate and implement leading customer relationship and messaging systems without locking you into a specific vendor. Our methodology preserves your data, segmentation, templates and automation workflows so you don’t lose momentum during the transition.

Deliverability is critical to email success. We set up domain authentication (SPF, DKIM and DMARC), configure dedicated IPs, plan warm‑ups and monitor sender health. We develop dynamic templates with multilingual and localisation frameworks as well as segmentation strategies and personalisation tokens to make every message relevant.

Beyond email, we build multi‑channel automation flows that nurture customers throughout their lifecycle - from welcome series and cart abandonment to post‑purchase, loyalty and win‑back programmes. We also define lead scoring models and lifecycle stages to align marketing, sales and service teams around the customer.

Key Benefits

  • Seamless migration of CRM and ESP platforms
  • Strong deliverability foundation with proper authentication
  • Personalised, multilingual and localised templates
  • Lifecycle automation across email, SMS and push channels
