Analytics & Reporting

Transform raw data into actionable insight. We build models, dashboards and attribution frameworks that help you measure and optimise performance.

What We Do

Data is only valuable when it drives decisions. We design and build data models and warehouses using platforms like BigQuery, Snowflake and Redshift, orchestrated with tools like dbt and Fivetran. On top of these foundations, we create interactive dashboards in Tableau, Power BI, Looker and Superset that surface key metrics across acquisition, engagement, retention, conversion, lifetime value and ROI.

Understanding how your campaigns influence revenue requires the right analytical frameworks. We implement multi‑touch attribution models, funnel analyses and segmentation insights that inform strategy and budget allocation. We also develop real‑time monitoring for campaign performance, deliverability and system health so you can react quickly when something changes.

Your team doesn’t need to be data scientists to use your data. We provide training and documentation so stakeholders can self‑serve analytics confidently. We also leverage built‑in reporting capabilities within your marketing platforms to track deliverability, open and click rates and customer journeys without leaving your primary systems.

Key Benefits

  • Centralise data in scalable warehouses and data models
  • Visualise performance with interactive dashboards
  • Measure true impact with attribution and funnel analysis
  • Enable teams to self‑serve insights and make data‑driven decisions
