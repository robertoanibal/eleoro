About Us

eleoro is led by a founder with more than 15 years building marketing operations and data-driven systems for big tech companies. Over time we learned what actually scales: clear processes, trustworthy data, and tools that make people faster rather than replacing them.

Today’s wave of AI finally lets us bring those lessons to everyday workflows. We build private AI assistants that work beside your team, not instead of them. They draft content, recommend next steps, connect data across tools, and surface insights. You stay in control at every step. Nothing goes live until a human approves it.

Our approach is hands-on. We start by understanding how your team works today, then we introduce assistants gradually so nothing breaks. We focus on outcomes you can feel: faster launches, fewer mistakes, clearer reporting, and more relevant customer experiences.

We also take privacy seriously. What our assistants learn for you stays with you. We do not use your data to train public models. Assistants begin in view-only mode and gain write access only with explicit approval. This keeps your brand voice consistent and your customers confident they are engaging with real people, supported by smart tools.

If you want modern results without losing the human element, that is what we build.

Privacy first: Your data and the learnings from your assistants remain private to your organization. Assistants start in view-only mode, propose changes, and require human approval before anything is applied. We do not use your data to train public models. The goal is simple: responsible AI that feels human and delivers results.
