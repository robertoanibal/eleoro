About Us

eleoro was founded to bridge the gap between marketing, technology and data. With over 15 years of experience across marketing technology, data architecture and campaign operations, our founder has designed enterprise martech stacks, unified customer data and launched campaigns reaching millions of customers.

Privacy first: your data, prompts, and embeddings never train public models. Agents run with least-privilege, start read-only, and generate dry-run change sets for approval.

From strategy and architecture to implementation and training, we’re with you every step of the way. Let’s build something extraordinary together.
