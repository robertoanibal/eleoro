About eleoro

eleoro is led by a founder with 15+ years building marketing operations and data systems for well known tech companies. We learned what scales in the real world: clear processes, trustworthy data, and tools that help people work faster instead of replacing them.

Today’s AI lets us bring those lessons to everyday work. We build private AI agents that connect with the tools you already use, handle routine tasks, recommend next steps, and surface insights. You stay in control at every step, and nothing publishes without human approval.

Our approach is hands on. We start by understanding how your business works today, then introduce agents gradually so nothing breaks. The goal is outcomes you can feel: faster launches, fewer mistakes, clearer reporting, and more relevant customer experiences.

How it works

Our agents integrate seamlessly with popular tools like HubSpot, Klaviyo, Outlook, WhatsApp, Google, QuickBooks, Facebook, and TikTok. We customize each agent or workflow to use the right language model for your business needs, or connect through automation tools when complex processes span multiple systems. This approach lets your business get started quickly, optimize what you already have, and deliver results without building everything from scratch.
