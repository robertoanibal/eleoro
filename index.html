Security & Trust

Your automations run on proven, secure platforms; including OpenAI’s Agent Builder, Zapier, Make, n8n, and Workato; trusted by thousands of businesses worldwide.

Data Ownership

You always own your data and workflows. Eleoro doesn’t store or resell your information; all data stays within your connected platforms such as Salesforce, HubSpot, or Shopify.

Encryption

All connections use TLS 1.2+ encryption in transit and AES-256 encryption at rest. For example, Zapier encrypts authentication tokens and Workato encrypts customer payloads before storage.

Compliance

Platforms like OpenAI’s Agent Builder, Zapier, Make, n8n, and Workato maintain SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, and CCPA compliance. HIPAA-eligible plans are available when required.

Access Control

Role-based permissions ensure that only authorised team members can modify workflows or credentials. Example: n8n supports per-user roles and Workato provides granular workspace access.

Audits

Each connected platform undergoes regular third-party security assessments and updates. You can view individual audit reports directly from each provider’s Trust or Security portal.

FAQ

Answers to common questions about configuring and running AI Agents with Eleoro.

How do I know which agents I need?+
We start with a short discovery call to map your processes; such as handling leads, managing orders, or automating follow-ups. Based on that, we recommend a small set of pre-built agents that match your tools (like HubSpot, Shopify, or QuickBooks). Each agent can later be customized as your workflows evolve.
How fast will I see results?+
Most small-business clients launch their first agents within 4–8 weeks. Pilot results; such as reduced manual tasks or faster response times; are usually visible within the first 30 days of deployment. Larger enterprise projects may include extended testing and approval phases.
Do I need any technical skills?+
No technical background is required. eleoro handles setup, integration, and testing on your preferred platforms. Once live, you’ll have access to an intuitive dashboard where you can monitor activity and request changes with a single click.
Is it secure?+
Yes. Your data stays within the platforms you already use. We build on trusted tools like OpenAI’s Agent Builder, Zapier, Make, n8n, and Workato; each offering enterprise-grade encryption, audit logs, and SOC 2 or GDPR compliance. Eleoro does not store or retain customer data.
Can I start small and expand later?+
Absolutely. Most clients begin with one or two agents, such as a Follow-Up or Orders Agent, and add more as they see results. Because every workflow runs on modular integrations, scaling to additional agents or departments is straightforward and low-risk.
Which tools and platforms do you support?+
We integrate with major business systems including HubSpot, Salesforce, Shopify, QuickBooks, Google Workspace, and Slack; plus over 5,000 apps supported by Zapier, Make, n8n, and Workato. During setup, we confirm compatibility and provide documentation for your exact tech stack.
What happens after launch?+
After deployment, you’ll receive a 30-day performance review to ensure your agents meet expected outcomes. We also offer optional monthly health checks and updates to keep automations aligned with your evolving processes.
Can you help with compliance or legal documentation?+
We provide a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) template for review and reference. Formal legal approval should be completed by your counsel. All connected platforms maintain published compliance certifications and can provide their respective reports on request.
